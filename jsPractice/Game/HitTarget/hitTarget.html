Hit The Target

Objective

Score as many points as you can by hitting the targets before time runs out!

How to play

  1. Start the Challenge: Once the game begins, you have 30 intense seconds to rack up as many points as possible.
  2. Targets on the Move: Every 2 seconds, a new target will pop up in a random spot on the play field. Keep your eyes sharp—these targets won’t wait around!
  3. Smash That Target: As soon as you see a target, click on it fast! Every hit scores you a point and brings you closer to victory.
  4. Missed It? No Problem: If you miss a target, don’t sweat it! Another one will appear in just a couple of seconds. Stay focused and keep clicking!
  5. Race Against the Clock: When the 30-second timer hits zero, the game is over. Your score will be displayed—will it be a new high score?

Hot tips

Stay on your toes! The targets move quickly, so be ready for anything.
